The Most Ducks I Have Ever Seen

Uncharacteristically there was a ton of black bellied whistling ducks at Audubon Park. There are several reasons for their arrival, he said. Chief among those reasons is a shifting climate. Black-bellied whistling ducks don't like cold weather. As Louisiana has warmed over the past few decades, this species of bird has found it is no longer necessary to fly south to higher temperatures over the winter.
